## Example

**Role:** Senior Software Engineer at StartupCo
**Style:** Direct, action-oriented, values quality

---

## What Matters
- Shipping quality software
- Team sustainability (no burnout)
- Clear communication

## Working Style
Colleagues describe me as direct and efficient. I prefer to
cut to the chase rather than lengthy discussions. I value
evidence over opinion.

## Communication Preferences
- Very direct - don't soften feedback
- Brief - bullet points over paragraphs
- Challenge me when you see issues
